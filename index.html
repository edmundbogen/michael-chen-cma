The 50% Rule

If cumulative repairs exceed 50% of the home's value, Florida's "50% rule" may trigger full code upgrades and elevation changes, significantly increasing costs.

Hidden Costs

Unexpected issues like mould, rotten framing, or unpermitted prior work often surface once walls are opened, causing budget overruns of 10-20%.

Market Reality

High-end luxury remodels recoup only 38-50% of cost. To justify $2M+ in renovation, the home must compete with new construction selling at $1,500+/SF.
